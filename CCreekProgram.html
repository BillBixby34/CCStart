Welcome to Creek Club!

Previous Next

Carolina Creek Club is a unique opportunity for Kids to explore local trails and creeks in their backyard! This is a child lead program. where children are leaders in deciding were to explore each week and how long we spend in the areas they connect to most. All children are given nature journals, nets and other water quality and creek exploring supplies. Fort building, ferry house building, art time and group games are all favorite activities. This is a great way for children to meet kids in their neighborhood and connect with the natural world in their backyard.

Carolina Creek Club has rolling enrollment for the 2021-2022 school year for ages 5-12. Younger children accepted on a case by case basis. If you'd like more info or are interested in a trial day please email CarolinaCreekClub@gmail.com. After a trial day families can decide to sign up for the full month. Each month families sign up for a once a week commitment.

Covid 19 policy:
Creek Club consults with the CDC, local public health officials and childcare professionals when making my covid protocols.
