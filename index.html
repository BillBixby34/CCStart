Welcome to Carolina Creek Club!

Carolina Creek Club has rolling enrollment for the 2021-2022 school year for ages 5-12. Younger children accepted on a case by case basis. If you'd like more info or are interested in a trial day please email CarolinaCreekClub@gmail.com. After a trial day families can decide to sign up for the full month. Each month families sign up for a once a week commitment.
