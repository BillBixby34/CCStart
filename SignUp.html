AFTERSCHOOL

We meet Wednesdays 2:45- 5:30 and Thursdays 3:45-5:45. This program is for 4-10 year olds. Activities each week vary, are weather dependent and include water quality investigations of Bolin Creek, stream stewardship skills, plant identification, games, and lots of physical activity. We will be outside exclusively. After you register, more details about what to bring and camp procedures will be shared with you.
